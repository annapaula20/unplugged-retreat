Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get to the Unplugged retreat?

Unplugged is accessible by train, plane, or car! Once you've made a reservation, our team will work with you to find the best method of transportation. We even offer a private coach to bring you the rest of the way from train station,and airport!

What kind of team is right for the Unplugged retreat?

We strongly believe in the power of unplugging therefore we are accept any group of 10 or more people who are looking to have a refreshing and productive work week!

What size teams can you accommodate?

Due to COVID, we have had to limit the amount of teams we can have at the same time. Please schedule a call with one of our facilitators to best options based on your team size.

Is there really no wifi available?

While wifi is not availble throughout the entire campus. We do offer computer rooms for when you need to connect with the outside world!
